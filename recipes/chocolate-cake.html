Chocolate Cake

Description

This recipe is for chocolate cake lovers who aren't keen on spending too much time in the kitchen (or for those who simply need to make a cake in a rush!)

The best part about this recipe is that you can get the whole thing done by using just a single bowl!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups flour
  • 3/4 cups cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Steps

  1. Mix the sugar, flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt evenly.
  2. Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla and mix for 2 minutes with an electric mixer.
  3. Stir in the boiling water.
  4. Pour the batter into a cake pan and bake for 30 minutes.
  5. Allow the cake to cool and enjoy!
