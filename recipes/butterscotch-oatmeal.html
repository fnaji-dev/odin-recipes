Butterscotch Oatmeal

Description

Ever get tired of having cereal or toast for breakfast? Why not mix it up by having some oatmeal that is not only healthier, but arguably more delicious?

This butterscotch oatmeal recipe won't take too much time in the morning, so fret not!

Ingredients

  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 tablespoons butter

Steps

  1. In a pan over medium heat, whisk together the egg, milk, and brown sugar.
  2. Add the oats to the pan and mix them in.
  3. As the oatmeal boils, stir until thick.
  4. Stir in the butter immediately upon removing from heat.
  5. Enjoy your warm and healthy breakfast!
