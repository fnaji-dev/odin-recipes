Reuben Sandwich

Description

Sandwiches are a timeless classic; everyone has had one (or many!) at some point in their life. The reuben is a sandwich that is less known in some places, though, so why not give it a try with this recipe?

With just a few simple steps, you'll be munching on your new favorite sandwich!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices rye bread
  • 1/2 cup Thousand Island dressing
  • 2 slices Swiss cheese
  • 2 slices deli corned beef
  • 1/4 cup sauerkraut
  • 1/2 tablespoon butter, softened

Steps

  1. Spread the dressing on each slice of bread.
  2. Add the cheese, beef, and sauerkraut on top of one slice of bread, then top with the other slice.
  3. Butter the outer side of each slice of bread.
  4. Place the sandwich on a hot skillet until the bottom is golden brown.
  5. Flip the sandwich, again waiting until golden brown.
  6. Chow down!
